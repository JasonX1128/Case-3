Fundamental constraints

1. Stop 3 (Redstone Medical Center) must be scheduled in the late afternoon. The contractual delivery commitment is 4:00 PM, so a morning delivery is not acceptable. Assume anything after 12:00 PM is fine for now.
2. Stop 14 (Sunrise Senior Living) cannot be served before 10:00 AM.
3. Any driver whose route overlaps midday must take one 30-minute lunch break between 11:30 AM and 1:30 PM.
4. Stop 5 (Construction Supply, 8617 Fourth St) must be served by a Small Van only. The Hillcrest Road area has a five-ton county road restriction, so Large Truck assignments are not allowed for this stop. It is likely that medium trucks are also not allowed but should be verified before being ruled out.
5. Under the current Hillcrest-road planning assumption, Stops 18, 19, 20, 21, and 22 must also be served by a Small Van only. Treat them as Hillcrest-access stops until a more precise road-access check is available.
6. Stop 27 (Redstone Elementary School, 2005 Walnut St) should be treated as an afternoon after-school-program delivery. For now, enforce it as not before 12:00 PM.
7. Main Street construction assumption: treat every solve as a Monday-through-Thursday construction day rather than a Friday. Add 20 minutes to any travel leg that goes to or from Stops 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6.
